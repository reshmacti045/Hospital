CRITICAL SERVICE

Mother Hospital Critical Care Expertise

Mother hospital is one of the best critical care hospital in Chittor.Critical care or Intensive care is a crucial medical specialty caring for patients.They may require support for instability (hypertension/hypotension), airway or respiratory compromise (ventilator support) Patients needing intensive/invasive monitoring, such as in the crucial hours after major surgery or patients who are considered too unstable to transfer to a less intensively monitored unit may also be placed in the intensive care units. The department of critical care medicine at Mother Hospital,India is a combination of many specialties and technologies, offering the possibility of survival to patients who are acutely and critically ill.

ICU

ICU management at Mother Hospital, India is based on the fact that methodical organization of Critical Care services influences important overall outcome measures such as mortality, length of stay and infection rates. Our 24-hour critical care units are a combination of multidisciplinary ICUS as well as ICUS dedicated to post cardiac surgery patients.At Mother Hospitals, a lot of emphasis is also placed on areas like nurses training, standardizing care through clinical pathways and the identification of ethical and economic issues pertaining to Critical Care making it the best critical care hospital in Chittor.
